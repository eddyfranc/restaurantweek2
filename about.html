About Us

Welcome to Pronto Restaurant, where fresh flavors and friendly service meet in a casual, vibrant setting. Established with the goal of offering a diverse menu that blends international favorites with local delicacies, we take pride in using high-quality ingredients to prepare dishes that satisfy every palate. Whether you’re in the mood for a hearty breakfast, a quick lunch, or a cozy dinner, Pronto promises an unforgettable dining experience with meals crafted to perfection.

At Pronto, we are committed to serving our community through various locations across Kenya, with restaurants conveniently situated in the bustling heart of Nairobi’s CBD and the scenic coastline of Mombasa. Our welcoming atmosphere and warm hospitality make us the perfect spot for individuals, families, and groups looking for a satisfying meal.

Join us and enjoy great food, memorable moments, and exceptional service every day at Pronto Restaurant. Your table is always ready!
